La réservation en split-screen

Palette · Prishtina Editorial

Typographie

Termini im
Fraunces (variable serif) · Instrument Sans (body) · Instrument Serif (italic emphasis).

Ton

Warm ivory base with burgundy as a primary accent — not generic purple. Gold is reserved for premium moments (gold CTA on dark, pending badge). Italic serifs italicize emotion; upright sans handles data. Grain overlay warms the whole canvas.
